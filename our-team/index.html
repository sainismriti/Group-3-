About Us!

We are a team of students at Harvard Kennedy School who are passionate about helping students like you choose the best schools. We all remember how difficult this decision is, and we wanted to use our experience to make this process a little bit easier.

Jake Steckler

Jake is a graduate student at Harvard Kennedy School and MIT Sloan School of Management, where he is pursuing an MPP and MBA. At Harvard, he works as a Research Assistant for the Technology and Public Purpose Project, where he studies the spread of misinformation on the internet.

He previously spent 8 years in the Army as a helicopter pilot after graduating from the United States Military Academy at West Point in 2014. Between his undergraduate degree and recent graduate school applications, he is passionate about making the application processes less stressful!

Jordan Callahan

Jordan is a current Mid-Career Master in Public Administration student at the Harvard Kennedy school. There, he is focused on data science, tech-for-good, and computer science. Previously, Jordan was a member in the U.S. Air Force, an Investment Banker at Barclays, and a Senior Program Manager at Amazon.

Additionally, Jordan also holds a Master of Business Administration from New York University's Stern School of Business, where he earned concentrations in Accounting, Banking, and Corporate Finance. He also holds a Master of Human Relations from the University of Oklahoma, and a Bachelor of Science in Liberal Arts - Psychology from Excelsior University. Jordan is well versed in navigating higher education!

Smriti Saini

Smriti is an MPA/ID candidate at Harvard Kennedy School. Prior to HKS, Smriti was assisting Delhi government on the mandate to combat tax evasion. She volunteers with initiatives to make education opportunities accessible for lower-caste and disadvantaged-group students in India.
